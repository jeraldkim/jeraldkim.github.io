Why Invest?

Investing is Essential

With Inflation Rates being reported as high as 8.8%,
money stored in a savings account would lose value overtime.

Investing is For Everyone

You don't have to start Big

You can start however small you want.

Add to it consistently over a long period of time and it will start to accumulate through the power ofcompounding.

Investing is Easy

You don’t have to research what stock to buy.

Instead buy the S&P 500 through ETFs like VOO, SPY. The S&P 500 is an index that tracks the Top 500 Biggest Companies in the US.

S&P 500 is Reliable

Since 1923, S&P 500 has made gains to this day in 2022,
averaging an annual return of 9.77%.
